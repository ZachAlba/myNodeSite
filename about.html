About Raiza

As a proud immigrant from the Philippines, I named my practice Kaya, which means "can" in Filipino. My goal as a Mental Health Counselor is to create a comfortable and safe environment for you to process your thoughts and feelings. I strive to empower every client, no matter what challenges they are faced with.


I specialize in adult clients struggling with relationship issues, anxiety, life transitions and self-confidence. As a therapist, I recognize that life doesn't always come easy. Relationships can be tricky and inevitable life changes can make us feel vulnerable and anxious. My style in sessions is to ask the right questions that lead to self exploration so that you can take better lead of your life. With compassion, I will help you shift unhelpful thinking patterns to ones that better serve you. In working with me, you can expect empathetic support along with appropriate challenges that will help move you forward in your journey.


I'm so glad you are considering this opportunity to heal and grow. I look forward to working with you!
